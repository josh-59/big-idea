Sequence of Abstractions

Suppose $X_1$ is a nonempty set, and let abstraction denote the "operation" of taking a proper subset (of a nonempty set). Then, it is a simple matter to describe a sequence of abstractions of $X_1$:

$$ X_1 \supset X_1' \supset X_1'' \supset X_1''' \supset ... \supset X_1^n$$

where $X_1^n = \varnothing$.

To implement this sequence, we require an abstracting function; a trivial one would not be difficult to implement. We require one that remembers, somehow, though...
