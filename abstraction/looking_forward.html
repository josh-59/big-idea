Looking Forward

My primary interest is in generating an artificial intelligence; along the way I've picked up too many ideas to develop them all to satisfaction.

Consciousness

We adopt the comparatively broad definition that a process is conscious of anything that it changes.

Order

A singular process need not execute in isolation. The order of a process is the minimum number of concurrently executing instances of itself. In particular, this affords the opportunity of self-consciousness.

Economy

One avenue for describing intelligence is simply the relative efficiency of a process, as compared to other processes. For instance, given two distinct cars, the one that is more fuel efficient is more intelligent than the other with respect to fuel.

Economization

A process is economizing if its execution alone implies an increase in efficiency; that is, learning is the first derivative of intelligence. Also note that learning is a process— it is consequence of a process which acts on itself. That is, learning implies self-consciousness.

Clever

A concept distinct from, though related to, intelligence, a process is clever if it looks ahead.

Creative and Optimizing

One (inelegant) approach to generating a learning process would involve a single process being alternatively creative (i.e., trying new things) and optimizing (i.e., discarding suboptimal solutions).

Deferring

This idea is inpired by this stack overflow answer (which is, in turn, references a linear algebra text by Michael Artin's). The quote is a beautiful rendition of the concept of ignoring, and I expect to see the "deferring" of less-interesting thoughts to be a useful concept.

Significance

Meaning strikes me as, "the difference it makes;" that is, significance is the change implied (by a given thing). It might be sufficient to clause significance within a conditional. My point is, if a given statement or artifact makes no change, then it has no significance, and has no meaning.

Fear

Caring about what is not known; caring about what is not observed; empty concern. Probably of more use within the context of processes / intellects.

Schizo

A more elegant approach to generating a learning process would be to implement an optimizing function that sees the same thing in two distict lights simultaneously. Thus, each "creative" and "optimizing" would occur as a result of the same process.

Intellect

The term intellect is handy to refer to an arbitrary individual's conscious working mind. I think that intellects are abstracting processes of order n > 1, so are self-conscious processes. In addition, intellects are "dimensional" processes, in the sense that each overarching abstraction is composed of a sequence of finer abstractions (of order possibly distinct from overarching order).
