Looking Forward

Memory profile

In order to implement intellect, we will require a method of defining semi-discrete (overlapping) abstractions of finite order. One method of defining the order of an abstraction would be to define their duration implicitly, as a consequence of a particular memory or "remembrance" profile:

Emotion

The "cost" of dereferencing abstractions; the "meaning" of belief. I think there's a connection between belief and emotion that, in part, guides the simplying process.

Thought

Definition: A thought is an ordered sequence of abstractions.

Since an intellect fields an indefinite sequence of abstractions, a thought is a "slice" of an intellect. It might be useful, at some point, to divide thought into two classes: Reasonable thought being free of contradictions, and unreasonable thought containing contradiction.

Reasonable thought has the important property that it may be abstracted away from.

An unreasonable thought has the equally important property that it may not be abstracted away from. As a conjecture, 'ego' or 'self' is an unreasonable thought.

Consciousness

Definition: A process is conscious if it effects change upon itself. That is, a process which is a process on itself is conscious.
