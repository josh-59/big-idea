Welcome

Hello,

This website presents an attempt to describe, in gory detail, the operation of the mind— specifically, the mind's intellect. The development will be concerned with the inalienable faculties of the mind, its tendency to organize, a detailed description of the structures of that organization, and justification of such organization.

I believe that the mind is principally defined by its memory, and the properties of that memory. Moreover, this development pursues the simplest possible explanation of the myriad complicated behaviors the mind is capable of.

Except where otherwise noted, all ideas presented here are my own. I've been working on these ideas for some time, and would like to put them into the public domain. Although I've done my best, I wish to consider each essay to be up for review; the purpose of the website is to motivate me to complete the exposition of these ideas, and to improve them through the criticism of others.

Enjoy!
