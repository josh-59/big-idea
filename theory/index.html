Theory

Introduction— We begin by defining the mind to be part of the body.

Abstraction— An abstraction is an ordered pair of two collections of information, $(a,b)$, such that $b$ is a proper subset of $a$.

Thought— A thought is a sequence of observations and abstractions which forms an abstraction.

Thread— A thread is a sequence of observations and abstractions acting upon a container of information such that the container starts empty, ends empty, and which includes a thought.

String— An string is a sequence of threads.
