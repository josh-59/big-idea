Thought and Abstraction

In common usage, the word "thought" has two distinct meanings. The first is found in the response to the question, "What are you thinking?" This equivalently asks things like, "Are you upset?", "Are you mad?", "Would you like to sit down?", etc.. The second usage is found in, "There's a thought!", or, "There's an idea!"
