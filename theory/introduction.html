Introduction

We begin our investigation by defining the mind to be part of the body:

For our purposes, the mind is composed of two parts: Memory and consciousness, where consciousness is the component of the mind which observes (writes to memory).

The development which follows will be concerned with the tendency of the mind to organize itself, the structures common in such organization, and quantification of the mind which will yield such structures.
